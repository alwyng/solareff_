Frequently Asked Questions
Answers to frequently asked questions
  • Is my location anonymous??

    Yes, when installing the application one would note that location services is not a requirement for our application. No information regarding the smart device's location is recorded or stored as part of the submission to Solareff.

  • How do I add custom rooms?

    Fill in the custom room's name into the text box just above the add room button on the configure rooms screen. Select the add room button and your custom room will be added to the default list of rooms.

  • How do I add appliances to each room?

    Each room has default appliances allocated to it. Simply complete the number of hours per day each respective appliances is used and how many of the respective appliances can be found in the selected room.

  • What if the appliance is not in the list?

    If there is an appliance in the room which is not in the default list of the room select the other category, fill in the name of the appliance as well as the hours per day and how many of these appliances are used in the selected room.

  • How do I submit my electricity bill?

    Select the "Take a photo of my electricity bill" button on the submission screen. This will launch the smart device's camera. Take a photo of your electricity bill. If the photo was slightly out of focus you can retry by selecting the retry button in the top left corner. Otherwise select OK to use the photo. You will be prompted to open your email application of choice, select it and an automatic draft with the photo already attached will be created. If you're happy with the information presented in the body of the email press send to submit to Solareff.
